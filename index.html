The HyperText Markup Language or HTML is the standard markup language for documents designed to be displayed in a web browser. It can be assisted by technologies such as Cascading Style Sheets and scripting languages such as JavaScript.

CSS 3

Cascading Style Sheets is a style sheet language used for describing the presentation of a document written in a markup language such as HTML or XML. CSS is a cornerstone technology of the World Wide Web, alongside HTML and JavaScript.

JAVA SCRIPT

JavaScript, often abbreviated JS, is a programming language that is one of the core technologies of the World Wide Web, alongside HTML and CSS. As of 2022, 98% of websites use JavaScript on the client side for web page behavior, often incorporating third-party libraries.

Bootstrap

Bootstrap is a free and open-source CSS framework directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development. It contains HTML, CSS and JavaScript-based design templates for typography, forms, buttons, navigation, and other interface components.

React JS

React is a free and open-source front-end JavaScript library for building user interfaces based on UI components. It is maintained by Meta and a community of individual developers and companies.

Why Every Business Needs A Website ?

Yes, every business needs a website here is the reason! Just Imagine, you invested some big amount and opened your own construction firm where you will provide all types of construction services in various cities. And also opened a new office and printed brochures, business cards and shared some with your family and friends. Yes, I agree you may get a few references also from friends. But the conversion rate is less. The one who actually looking for the exact service you are providing will be searching on the internet. They will be browsing for the best construction company in Bangalore or your city name, near me, or with certain other features. Ok fine! you have added your company google my business page and you gave a phone number too where people can contact you. But if he is searching at midnight and your working hours is till 7 or 8 pm then he will never call you. Even if he made a decision to call you in the morning, then he definitely wants to know what kind of work you do? what are the works you have already done? Will you also provide interior design service? Now people want proof of your work, before availing it. What if you own a website for your construction firm and mention in-detailed services list on the website. What kind of works do you do, your completed projects ongoing projects. Then a customer who was searching for this service looks into your website and gets some trust and there will be a high chance of getting construction work from your company. Website is all about building trust with your customers, proving your existence in the current market. And connecting to them at any time and anywhere.
